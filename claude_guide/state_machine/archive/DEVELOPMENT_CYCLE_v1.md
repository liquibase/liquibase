# Team Development Cycle

## Master Development Loop

This is the core development cycle the team follows for every feature or task. For each phase, assume the appropriate role and consult that role's documents for specific patterns and processes.

### The Five Phases

1. **Requirements** → Product Owner role
   - Define clear acceptance criteria
   - Ensure requirements are specific and measurable
   
2. **Implementation** → Developer role  
   - Follow established patterns
   - Build the feature according to requirements
   
3. **Test** → QA role
   - Verify functionality works as specified
   - Test with real environments
   
4. **Document** → Technical Writer role
   - Document the implemented feature
   - Update guides with new patterns
   
5. **Retrospective** → Scrum Master role
   - Facilitate team learning
   - Capture what to CELEBRATE/IMPROVE/STOP

### How to Use This Cycle

1. **Identify current phase** - Where are you in the cycle?
2. **Assume appropriate role** - Switch to the role for that phase
3. **Read role documents** - Check `/roles/[role]/` for patterns and processes
4. **Execute the phase** - Complete the work for that phase
5. **Move to next phase** - Continue until cycle complete

### Key Principle

Each role owns their phase and has specific deliverables. No phase is complete until its deliverable exists and meets quality standards. The cycle ensures systematic progress while maintaining quality and learning.
